University Library Rich & well-stocked Central & School Libraries with more than 2,00,000 Books, Periodicals and Journals in print as well as in electronic form with online subscription of IEEE, INDEST, Science Direct, Bentham etc. Auditorium The university campus boasts of an ultra modern air-conditioned auditorium with a seating capacity of 1000.
